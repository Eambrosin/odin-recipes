Brazilian Barbecue (Churrasco)

Brazilian Barbecue

Description

Churrasco is a traditional Brazilian barbecue where various cuts of meat are grilled over an open flame. It's a popular dish in Brazil, often served with rice, beans, and farofa.

Ingredients

  • Various cuts of beef (like picanha or flank steak)
  • Salt to taste
  • Garlic cloves (optional)
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Season the meat with salt and garlic (optional).
  2. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  3. Grill the meat on the skewers for about 10-15 minutes, turning occasionally.
  4. Serve with rice, beans, and farofa (Brazilian cassava flour).
