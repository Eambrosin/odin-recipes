Lasagna

Description

This is a traditional lasagna recipe with rich bolognese sauce, cheese, and layers of pasta. Lasagna is an Italian dish loved by many. It combines layers of tender pasta, savory sauce, and melted cheese, making it a perfect comfort food.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Bolognese sauce (made from ground beef, tomatoes, and herbs)
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Herbs (basil, oregano, etc.)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large pan, sauté garlic and onion until soft. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Add tomato sauce, bolognese sauce, and herbs to the pan. Simmer for 15 minutes to combine flavors.
  5. Spread a thin layer of sauce on the bottom of a baking dish. Add a layer of noodles, followed by sauce, ricotta cheese, mozzarella, and Parmesan. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of cheese on top.
  6. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 30 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  7. Let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
