Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat, cheese, and a rich tomato sauce. It’s a comfort food loved by many and can be customized with various ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Herbs (basil, oregano, etc.)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  3. In a pan, sauté the garlic and onion, then add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Add tomato sauce and herbs to the meat mixture, then simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Layer the lasagna in a baking dish: start with sauce, then noodles, cheese, and repeat.
  6. Bake in the oven for 30 minutes, until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
