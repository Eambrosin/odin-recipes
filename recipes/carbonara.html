Pasta Carbonara

Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Pasta Carbonara is an Italian classic made with egg, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. It's a rich and creamy dish thatâ€™s perfect for any pasta lover.

Ingredients

  • 200g Pasta (spaghetti or fettuccine)
  • 100g Pancetta
  • 2 large Eggs
  • 1 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water until al dente.
  2. In a pan, cook the pancetta until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk eggs, cheese, salt, and pepper.
  4. Drain the pasta and combine it with pancetta and egg mixture, tossing well to create a creamy sauce.
  5. Serve with extra grated Parmesan.
